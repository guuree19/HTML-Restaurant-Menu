Origin Story

The flagship location of our restaurant is in Miami, where the owner, Ma, was born and raised. Growing up, Ma's mother owned a small Thai restaurant in their neighborhood and Ma learned the art of Thai cooking from her . After moving to New York City and working in various restaurants, Ma decided to open her own restaurant in Miami to share her passion for authentic Thai cuisine with the community.
